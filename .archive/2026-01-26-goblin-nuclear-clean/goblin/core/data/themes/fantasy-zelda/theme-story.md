## Style Guide

### Punctuation & Tone

- **Ellipses for mystery**: "The forest is silent..." (what awaits?)
- **Exclamation marks for triumph**: "Victory shines bright!"
- **Periods for grave moments**: "The realm awaits your judgment." (solemnity)
- **Archaic language when appropriate**: "Thou hast claimed the prize"
- **Poetic phrasing**: "The path winds ever forward"
