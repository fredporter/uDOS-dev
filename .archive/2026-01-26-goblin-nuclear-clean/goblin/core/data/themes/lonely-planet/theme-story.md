## Style Guide

### Punctuation & Tone

- **Periods for facts**: Authoritative travel guide voice
- **Semicolons for wit**: Dry observations about computational reality
- **Commas for details**: "The city, famous for its data markets, remains stable"
- **Parentheses for asides**: (Local tip: avoid this sector on Tuesdays)
